The problem

Great content doesn't guarantee
a great presentation

Even experienced speakers struggle with real-time delivery. The cognitive load of managing slides, notes, timing, audience attention, and live Q&A simultaneously degrades performance.

Pacing breaks down

Adapting the same content to 15, 30, or 60 minutes requires complete rework every time.

Q&A disrupts flow

Audience questions break your narrative rhythm. You lose your place and struggle to get back on track.

Engagement drops unnoticed

By the time you realize your audience has checked out, the damage is already done.

Features

Not a teleprompter.
A speaking copilot.

Pretive doesn't just show you a script. It understands your content, follows your delivery, and actively helps you perform better.

Document to Narrative

Upload PDFs, docs, URLs, or notes. Pretive builds a structured narrative with automatic 5/15/30/60 minute delivery modes.

AI Prompter

Context-aware prompts that adapt to your speaking pace. See your next key point, not a wall of text.

Live Speaking Copilot

Q&A Orchestrator

Recovery Mode

Post-Session Intelligence
