The problem

You know the content.
But delivery is a
different skill.

Even the most experienced speakers face cognitive overload during live presentations. Slides, notes, timing, Q&A, audience energy — managing all of it at once degrades your performance.

Pacing breaks down

Adapting content to 15, 30, or 60 minutes requires complete rework.

Q&A disrupts flow

Audience questions break your rhythm and you lose your thread.

Engagement drops

By the time you notice the audience checked out, it's too late.

Capabilities

Not a teleprompter.
Not a slide maker.
A presentation orchestrator.

Narrative Engine

Upload PDFs, docs, URLs, slides, or raw notes. Pretive extracts the core narrative and generates structured delivery plans in 5, 15, 30, or 60-minute formats — with transitions, key messages, and Q&A anchor points.

Semantic Prompter

Not a scrolling script. Pretive tracks meaning, not words — pausing when you pause, tolerating improvisation, and guiding you back if you skip ahead.

Live Speaking Copilot

Silent nudges only you see: "give an example here", "wrap up", "slow down", "move to CTA". Like a director in your earpiece.

Q&A Orchestrator

Classifies incoming questions, suggests which to answer now vs. defer, and generates source-grounded response suggestions in real time.

Recovery Mode

Lost your thought? Say "simplify this", "give me three sentences", or "switch to technical" — instant rephrase without missing a beat.

Screen Intelligence

Simplify cluttered slides on the fly, magnify key data, insert support cards, and dynamically adapt what's on screen to your narrative.

Dynamic Augmentation

Say "show a timeline" or "give a counter-example" mid-talk. Pretive generates it live — comparisons, summaries, historical examples, anything.

Session & Team Memory

Every session builds institutional knowledge — Q&A pools, approved narratives, session libraries. Multiple trainers deliver the same content at a consistent quality bar. Your organization gets smarter with every talk.

Live Research & Media

Pull supporting videos, articles, case studies, or knowledge base entries into your live session without breaking flow.

Post-Session Intel

Auto-generated summaries, action items, follow-up emails, and performance insights after every talk.
