Cover Letter
============

Dear Reviewer,

I am applying with PRETIVE, a real-time AI copilot designed to support live
teaching and presentation delivery.

The project is built on years of hands-on experience in online education. Since
2012, our team has operated one of Turkey's largest live education platforms,
serving over 300,000 learners across thousands of live sessions. PRETIVE
addresses a clear and recurring problem we experienced firsthand: managing live
delivery effectively while maintaining content quality.

Unlike traditional tools that focus on content creation (before the session) or
meeting summarization (after the session), PRETIVE operates during live sessions
and provides contextual support in real time — transition guidance, timing
suggestions, context cards, and Q&A orchestration.

We have developed a functional MVP and are currently running controlled pilot
sessions with early instructors. The core real-time delivery flow has been
validated through 20+ live sessions.

The product is designed as a globally scalable SaaS platform with a clear
subscription-based business model. It works exclusively on user-provided
content, does not perform biometric or emotion detection, and is designed with
EU AI Act compliance in mind.

The goal is to build a scalable software product with global reach, starting
with online education and expanding into broader professional communication use
cases.

I believe PRETIVE fits the definition of an innovative and scalable startup and
has strong potential for growth in the rapidly expanding EdTech and AI-assisted
delivery market.
