PRETIVE — Startup Summary
==========================

PRETIVE is a real-time AI copilot designed specifically for live online
instructors and educators.

The product solves a fundamental problem in live teaching: managing content,
timing, structure, and audience interaction simultaneously.

Unlike presentation tools or meeting assistants, PRETIVE operates during the
session and supports delivery in real time.


Product
-------

PRETIVE analyzes speech in real time, matches it with structured content, and
provides contextual support such as:

  - Transition guidance
  - Timing suggestions
  - Context cards (summaries, comparisons, key ideas)
  - Q&A orchestration

The system works only on user-provided and approved content, ensuring controlled
and reliable output. All AI-generated content is clearly labeled.


Innovation
----------

The core innovation is a real-time pipeline:

  speech -> meaning -> decision -> screen

This enables dynamic delivery support instead of static content generation.

PRETIVE does not generate content blindly. It works on structured content the
instructor provides and real-time context from their speech. No uncontrolled
generation.


Market
------

Initial focus:
  - Online instructors and course creators
  - Webinar creators and producers
  - Corporate training professionals
  - Bootcamp instructors

The product expands later into broader professional communication use cases.


Current Status
--------------

PRETIVE has a functional MVP and is currently in private beta.

  - 20+ live sessions tested with real instructors
  - Early pilot users onboarded
  - Real-time delivery flow validated
  - Core system built and working
  - Feedback loop in progress

We are currently working with a limited number of users to refine the
experience before broader release.


Origin
------

PRETIVE is built on top of years of real-world live education experience.
The founding team operated IIENSTITU, one of Turkey's largest online education
platforms, since 2012. Over 300,000 learners participated in live sessions.

Every feature in PRETIVE maps directly to a real operational pain point
experienced during those years of live teaching.


Business Model
--------------

SaaS subscription with fair-usage model:

  - Pro:        EUR 19/month (individual instructors)
  - Team:       EUR 24/seat/month (academies, bootcamps, small teams)
  - Enterprise: EUR 45+/seat/month (corporate L&D, large organizations)

Each plan includes a fair-use allowance of live session hours, document storage,
and AI processing. Overages are billed per unit.


Responsible AI
--------------

  - Works exclusively on user-provided content
  - No biometric data processing or emotion detection
  - Human-in-the-loop: all AI suggestions are advisory, instructor retains
    full control
  - Transparent AI labeling on all generated content
  - Designed with EU AI Act compliance in mind


Scalability
-----------

PRETIVE is a globally scalable software product with recurring revenue
potential. The technology stack is built for multi-tenant SaaS from day one.
No per-customer customization required.


Conclusion
----------

PRETIVE is not a content generation tool.

It is a real-time delivery intelligence system designed to improve how knowledge
is presented and understood — during the live moment of teaching.
