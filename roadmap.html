Product Roadmap

Five phases from MVP to platform

A disciplined 36-month product plan that takes Pretive from a single-user copilot to a full presentation intelligence platform, with clear milestones and measurable KPIs at every stage.

Phase 1 Month 0 – 12

Core MVP

Build the foundational intelligence loop. Content ingestion from Drive and OneDrive feeds a narrative engine that generates pre-session drafts. A semantic prompter drives live speech tracking while basic screen intelligence and real-time fact-checking ensure accuracy. Every session closes with an automated summary. Ship Zoom and Google Meet integrations to reach users where they already present.

Key deliverables
Content ingestion Pre-session draft generation Narrative engine Semantic prompter Live speech tracking Basic screen intelligence Basic fact-check Session summary
Integrations
Zoom Google Meet Google Drive OneDrive
Target KPIs
150 paying accounts €8K MRR Weekly active 60%+ Zoom + Meet marketplace approval NPS ≥ 40
Phase 2 Month 13 – 18

Controlled Live Augmentation

Graduate from passive guidance to active augmentation. An autonomous decision engine and background agent system work together to surface comparison cards, media additions, and real-time overlays without breaking the presenter's flow. A question engine and command-based expansion give presenters superpowers during live Q&A.

Key deliverables
Autonomous decision engine Background agent system Question engine Command-based expansion Comparison cards Media additions Online overlay
Integrations
Microsoft Teams Webex
Target KPIs
400 accounts €20K MRR Teams integration Monthly churn < 6% 10+ enterprise pilots
Phase 3 Month 19 – 24

Participant Platform

Flip the model from single-user to multi-sided. Each participant gets their own AI agent, interactive quizzes and surveys, and personal notes that persist after the session ends. For organizations, a corporate workspace with admin panel and interaction analytics turns presentation data into actionable insight. LMS integrations via LTI 1.3 and webinar platform connections expand distribution.

Key deliverables
Participant agent Interactive quiz / survey Personal notes Corporate workspace Admin panel Interaction analytics
Integrations
LMS (LTI 1.3) Webinar platforms
Target KPIs
800 accounts €50K MRR 5,000+ active participants 2+ LMS integrations Participant viral coefficient ≥ 1.1
Phase 4 Month 25 – 30

Adaptive Intelligence

Move from generic assistance to personalized intelligence. The system learns each presenter's teaching style, autonomously manages presentation flow, and builds team-wide memory across sessions. A complete audit log and advanced analytics package makes Pretive enterprise-ready for regulated industries.

Key deliverables
Teacher style learning Autonomous presentation mgmt Team memory Audit log Advanced analytics
Target KPIs
1,200 accounts €80K MRR SOC 2 Type II Enterprise churn < 3% Net Revenue Retention ≥ 110%
Phase 5 Month 31 – 36+

Platform Layer

Open the engine to the ecosystem. A public API and SDK let third parties build on Pretive's real-time intelligence. Deeper enterprise controls, whitelabel options, and live delivery infrastructure transform the product from a standalone tool into presentation infrastructure that other products are built on top of.

Key deliverables
API / SDK Third-party integrations Deeper enterprise controls Live delivery infrastructure
Target KPIs
€1M+ ARR 20+ API customers Monthly positive cash flow Gross margin 85%+ 3+ whitelabel deals
Technical Validation

The first 90 days

Three focused sprints to validate the hardest technical assumptions before writing a single line of product code.

Day 0 – 30

Speech-to-Graph Matching PoC

Prove that spoken words can be mapped to presentation graph nodes in real time. This is the foundational capability every other feature depends on.

Target: 85%+ position accuracy
Day 30 – 60

Live Pipeline on Zoom SDK

Stress-test the full real-time pipeline end-to-end on Zoom's SDK. Latency and uptime at production thresholds are non-negotiable for a live copilot.

Target: <1.5s end-to-end, 90%+ uptime
Day 60 – 90

Decision Quality Validation

Put the decision engine in front of 10 real teachers and measure whether interventions are genuinely helpful. User perception is the ultimate benchmark.

Target: 70%+ rated "useful", false positive <5%
Technical difficulty = Defensibility. Real-time narration intelligence requires orchestrating multiple AI disciplines—STT, NLU, decision theory, real-time render—simultaneously. This complexity is what keeps general-purpose platforms from solving it in a weekend hackathon.
